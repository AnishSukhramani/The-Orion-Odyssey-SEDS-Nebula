About the Event

Hey there space enthusiasts, get ready to traverse through the expanse of the universe, as we unveil The Orion Odyssey - July Symposium; a truly mind-blowing event that will leave you in awe! Mark your calendars for July 24th - 25th, as we gather for an immersive experience like no other. Prepare to be captivated in an engaging platform for all astrophysics enthusiasts to exchange knowledge, ideas, and discoveries.

Join us on this cosmic adventure and expand your understanding of the universe!



About Us

SEDS Nebula

SEDS (Students for the Exploration and Development of Space) is an international organization aiming to promote space exploration and development by involving the educational sector through many school and engineering projects. We, SEDS Nebula are one of its chapters at VIT Bhopal University. Our aim is to create awareness about space exploration activities by involving students and making them realize where their true passion lies by means of projects, outreach, space talks, space events, competitions, visits and other engaging activities. Our aim is not just to raise a new generation of inquisitive learners but also the innovative shapers of the future society who will in turn inspire others to follow and learn more about their dreams.
